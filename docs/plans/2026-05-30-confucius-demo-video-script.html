4 · Why

The problem it solves

0:45 – 1:00 (15 s)

Every day you lose hours — walking, training, commuting. You can't interactively learn with your phone while in motion.

Books

Need your eyes. Can't read while you move.

YouTube

Talks at you. Can't ask a follow-up.

Podcasts

Linear. Scrubbing back kills the flow.

With Confucius, everyone can learn — anywhere, anytime.

Capture audio for real

Record the demo against the deployed learn-bot. Don't script the bot's lines word-for-word — let the real model output drive the cut. The Q and follow-up are the only things you stage.

Never show the phone screen

Phone stays in the pocket / on the armband the entire video. The point lands the second a viewer realizes the screen never came on.

Captions on, music off

Burn-in captions in two colors (you / bot). No music under the demo turn — the bot's voice is the product.

If over time

Trim the bot's first answer by one sentence. Never trim the follow-up — the follow-up is the whole interactive argument.
