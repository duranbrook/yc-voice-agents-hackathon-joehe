Cekura dashboard · demo script

~30-second screen recording. Narrator VO over a live click-through. Drop into the demo video's 0:32 – 0:45 beat or expand to a standalone demonstration.

step 010–4 s

Open Sessions tab

→ default view · 10 sessions visible

VO: "This is every conversation Confucius has had in the last week — pulled straight from Pipecat tool events into Cekura."

step 024–9 s

Hover over the 4 stat cards

→ Sessions 24h · comprehension · time-to-understand · bounce-back

VO: "Every session is measured. Comprehension rate. Time-to-understanding. How many users come back to a topic they didn't finish."

step 039–15 s

Click session-002 · compound interest

→ drills into detail view

VO: "Drill into one session. You get the phase timeline, every concept the bot covered, every thing the user wanted to come back to."

step 0415–19 s

Scroll to Tool calls + Latency

→ shows set_topic / add_concept / mark / recap / end_session counts + p50/p95

VO: "Five Pipecat tool events per session. Cekura groups them so you can see exactly when each one fired and how fast the bot was."

step 0519–24 s

Click Metrics tab

→ jump to aggregate view

VO: "Zoom out. Time-to-understanding is dropping — our prompt fix last week cut it by twelve seconds."

step 0624–30 s

Click Trending tab

→ topic chart + marked-for-later

VO: "And we see what everyone's stuck on. 'Back-prop intuition' gets marked-for-later three times this week. That's a backlog of demand we can turn into pre-built explainers."

closingend card

VO (close): "Voice is the input. Cekura turns it into a product feedback loop."
